Inspirational Women

Who want to help

Many women have gone through domestic violence or sexual abuse and they are here to other women who are going through the same thing by sharing their stories and to hopefully inspire others to reach out for help.

Halle Berry


Halle Berry claims that one of her life's purpose is to help others especially women after her experience with domestic abuse. She witnessed her own mother getting beaten for many years of her life and she says "I have an understanding, a knowing and thats what connects me to this organization.I feel like I have something that I can impart to these women. It seems like I've oversome but I really haven't.In the quiet of my mind,I struggle."

Robin Givens


After hearing the suspension of the former NFL player Ray Rice for the assault on his fiancee she saw the video of him actually punching Janay Rice and it reminded her of her past.She was carried out of her finace's apartment and into a car after being beaten by him.She was told by her fiancee that she had bounced off the walls however,at that time she was engaged to him but living with her mother but wasn't able to go back since she didn't want to explain what had happened to her."I was embarassed to tell my own mother what he had done to me I even blamed myself for this what had happened.I later learned that it was not my fault but my mistake for not leaving him when he first raised his hand at me." She later divoreced him and she has been working on behalf of women, children and families facing through domestic violence for 20 years now.
