Cry Out Resources

Statistic

  • 1 in 3 women have been victims of domestic violence.
  • Women ages 18-24 are most likely to be abused by a partner.
  • Domestic violence causes higher depression and suicide rates.
  • 19% of domestic violence includes a weapon.
  • 90% of rape victims are female.
  • There are more than 20,000 people who place calls to hotlines for domestic violence daily.
  • 1 in 5 women have gotten raped in their lifetime.
  • 17.7 million women have been raped since 1998.
  • First passed by Congress in 1984, the Family Violence Prevention and Services Act (FVPSA) is the primary federal funding stream dedicated to the support of emergency shelter and related assistance for victims of domestic violence and their children.
  • Nearly half of all women and men in the United States have experienced psychological aggression by an intimate partner in their lifetime.
  • Females ages 18 to 24 and 25 to 34 generally experienced the highest rates of intimate partner violence.
  • 40% of women who have been sexually abused were sexually abused as children.
