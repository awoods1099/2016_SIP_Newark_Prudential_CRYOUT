We Want To Help

Welcome to our Page! We Created this page to provide recources for women who has been abused sexually or physically in a relationship. We really wanted to show Women that there is hope on the other side and that they can overcome their sitaution no mater what the world throws at them. We would try our best to reach out to underground shelters for abused women and hotlines so they can get help as soon as possible. If you are in need of Shelters Asap please go to our Resources page to find the nearest Shelter near you. If you are afraid to move on your phone please call those Hotlines or CALL 911! PLEASE !
